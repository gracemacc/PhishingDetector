How to Export Email Files

  1. Open the suspicious email
  2. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Show original"
  4. Click "Download Original"
  5. Save the .eml file to your computer

  1. Open the suspicious email
  2. Click "File" in the menu
  3. Select "Save As"
  4. Choose "Outlook Message Format" (.msg)
  5. Save the file to your computer

Alternative: Right-click the email → "View Source" → Copy and paste into our text input.

  1. Open the suspicious email
  2. Click the "More" button (three dots)
  3. Select "View Raw Message"
  4. Copy the entire source code
  5. Paste it into our text input field

  5. Paste it into our text input field

How Our Detection Works

Header Analysis

Checks email headers for spoofing, authentication failures, and suspicious routing.

Sender Verification

Analyzes sender information for typosquatting, free email abuse, and domain anomalies.

Link Reputation

Checks all URLs against Google Safe Browsing and VirusTotal databases.
